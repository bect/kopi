Understanding the structure of your Hugo project is crucial for efficient development.

This theme follows standard Hugo conventions. The `assets` folder contains your SCSS and JavaScript, while `layouts` defines the HTML structure.

Content is organized in the `content` directory, mirroring the URL structure of your rendered site.
